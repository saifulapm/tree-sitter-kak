==================
Simple define-command
==================

define-command my-command %{echo hello}

---

(program
  (define_command
    name: (command_name
      (word))
    body: (percent_string
      content: (string_content))))

==================
Define-command with switches
==================

define-command -override -params 1 -docstring 'say hi' my-command %{echo hello}

---

(program
  (define_command
    (switch)
    (switch
      (word))
    (switch
      (single_quoted_string
        (string_content)))
    name: (command_name
      (word))
    body: (percent_string
      content: (string_content))))
